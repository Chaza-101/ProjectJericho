    • Project Jerico: Walking Tour.

    Alex Monzon 2025

    This experience will take you through a block of the city, ending at the art institute. Along the way, you can tap on the icons popping up to learn fun facts about the city.

    This project is location-based, making it an idea for augmented reality. It allows us to interact with the world creatively, educating people about Chicago while also letting them see it in person, and leading them to a location where they can learn even more. I ended up needing to scale down my project a lot, but I am happy with the outcome.
