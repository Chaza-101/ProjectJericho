Chicago's Official Flower!

Did you know that chicago's official flower is the Violet? The flower has long been connected to qualities that are very close to the city's soul, such as modesty, loyalty, and perseverance. Like the violet itself, Chicagoans have demonstrated a remarkable ability to overcome hardship, from overcoming the Great Chicago Fire of 1871 to confronting the problems of contemporary urbanization.
